Summary For Wind Speed

No correlation appears to exist in either hemisphere for wind speed and latitude. Air in the atmosphere moves around the globe in a pattern called global atmospheric circulation and this pattern of atmospheric circulation, is caused by the sun heats the earth more at the equator than at the poles and it is also affected by the spin of the earth.
