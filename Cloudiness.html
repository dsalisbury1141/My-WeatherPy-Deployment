City Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Measurements of cloudiness vary widely across latitudes. Measurements tend to cluster more so at certain percentages such as, 0%, 50% and 75% if we were in a space ship it would appear that clouds dominate the earth. In this analysis we can see that there is no correlation between latitude and cloudiness.
